My Story


I started my memory with a pencil and an eraser in my hands. Being a stray artist was my dream.

In college, I fell in love with computers, surprisingly, and decided to take a leap of faith and studied Computer Science instead. Being able to program gave me the benefit of not just designing something beautiful but also building them. After graduating from college, I joined Facebook as a software engineer. I met many interesting people in the bay area and decided to start a studio to build things together.

As relentless as we are, LZStudio has created many useful products in the past years: Starry, Vocapp, Dinnr and so on.  And we cannot wait to meet and team up with more awesome teams to bring some world-changing products alive.

Starry


Problem
Normally when someone wants to know funding/traffic information of a startup, s/he will have to go to three different websites: CrunchBase, AngelList and Alexa, which becomes very annoying if you do so frequently.

Solution
A chrome/safari extension that integrates these three datasources and helps investors and entrepreneurs lookup startup information with one click. Supports for more platform are coming soon.

Vocapp


Problem
Constantly interrupted by unfamiliar words while reading? Seen a word several times but still can't recognize it? Tired of studying tedious definition lists?

Solution
A chrome extension that helps people lookup dictionary with one click, and utilizes the contextual learning and the forgetting curve to review vocabulary lists with maximum efficiency.
